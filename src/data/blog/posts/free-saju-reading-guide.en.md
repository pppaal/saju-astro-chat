## What Is Saju?

Saju (사주), also called the Four Pillars of Destiny, is a traditional East Asian system that maps the energy of your birth into four "pillars" — year, month, day, and hour. Each pillar has a Heavenly Stem and an Earthly Branch drawn from the Five Elements (wood, fire, earth, metal, and water). Together they form a personal blueprint that practitioners read for personality, strengths, relationships, and timing.

## What You Need

To calculate your Saju accurately, you need three things:

- **Birth date** — solar or lunar; a good calculator converts automatically.
- **Birth time** — as exact as possible, because the hour pillar depends on it.
- **Birth place** — used for time-zone and longitude adjustments.

If you don't know your exact birth time, you can still get a useful reading from the year, month, and day pillars. The hour pillar simply adds more nuance.

## How to Read Your Saju for Free, Step by Step

1. **Get your Four Pillars calculated.** An online Saju calculator turns your birth data into the eight characters (사주팔자) of the four pillars.
2. **Find your Day Master.** The Heavenly Stem of your day pillar represents *you* — your core self. Everything else is read in relation to it.
3. **Look at the Five Elements balance.** Which elements are strong, and which are missing? Balance and imbalance hint at natural strengths and the areas you may want to develop.
4. **Read the relationships between pillars.** Elements that support or clash with your Day Master shape how you experience people, work, and timing.
5. **Apply it to a real question.** Saju is most useful when connected to something concrete — a decision, a relationship, or the year ahead.

## Common Questions

**Is Saju the same as a horoscope?** No. A Western horoscope is usually based on your sun sign alone. Saju uses your full birth time and a completely different elemental framework.

**Do I need to believe in it?** Treat it as a lens for reflection. Many people use Saju to understand their tendencies and to time decisions — not to predict a fixed future.

**Is a free reading good enough?** A free calculation gives you the accurate raw chart. The value is in the interpretation: connecting the pillars to your actual life.
